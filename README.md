## Philosophy

Most "AI agent" tutorials show you a chatbot and call it a day. The Dojo is different.

By Week 4, your agent has memory. By Week 8, you have two agents coordinating work. By Week 12, you have infrastructure that runs autonomously across machines.

Every lesson ends with something working. No dead theory. No "imagine if." You build it, it runs, you move to the next belt.
